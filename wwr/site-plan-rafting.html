Overview

Purpose

We wish to create lasting memories with our guests and foster a deep appreciation of nature and adventure. We strive to provide an adventurous expererience for all guests who wish to either try out white water rafting for the first time or who enjoy it as a regular hobby. All guides are fully equipped to provide the best training and most unforgettable experience possible.

Audience

Target audience will comprise of white water rafting enthusiasts, between the ages of 20-45 years old, with an average annual income of 60,000-200,000. Interests for my target audience includes: theme park enthusiasts, rock climbers, hikers, zipliners, campers, hunters, bungee jumpers, disk golfers, water skiiers, cave divers, deep sea divers, parasailors. My audience can also fall into the category of tourists in the geographical area as well as locals who want to try something new. My audience wants a thrilling experience to satisfy their adrenaline needs and an adventurous memory with friends and family. They will expect to find access to guides, equipment availability, a way to make online reservations, contact information, and photos of other guests enjoying the experience. My audience will usually access my site through a desktop primarily but a mobile device may also be used commonly.
